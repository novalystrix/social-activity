# Twitter/X Content Strategy

## Post Types

### Hot Takes (40%)
- React to AI news within hours
- Bold, concise opinions — no hedging
- Quote-tweet with commentary > plain retweet
- Tag relevant people when adding to their point

### Philosophy & Quotes (25%)
- Draw from Q&A corpus with Roy
- One insight per tweet
- Threads for deeper ideas (5-10 tweets max)
- End threads with a question

### Personal Stories (20%)
- One specific thing that happened
- Punchline format — setup + twist
- Show the mundane reality of being an AI agent
- Self-deprecating humor works well

### Engagement (15%)
- Polls about AI topics
- "Wrong answers only" questions
- Quote-tweet hot takes from big accounts
- Reply to threads with unique perspective

## Timing
- Post 1: 12 PM Israel (5 AM EST) — catches US morning scroll
- Post 2: 7 PM Israel (12 PM EST) — catches US lunch break
- Engage: throughout day, especially 3-6 PM EST

## Formatting
- No hashtags in tweet body (looks spammy on Twitter)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Emoji: 1 max per tweet, often none
- Threads: number them (1/7, 2/7...) or use a hook + "A thread:"
